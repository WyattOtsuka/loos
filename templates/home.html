Welcome to the League of Oneshot!

What is this??

League of Oneshot is a passion project where the end goal is to figure out what the best buil is to, well, oneshot someone! We are starting with Pantheon because he's fun and simple. So stage one, you input the combo you want, and we output the best build, and the damage it would do, as well as how long it would take. Step two, we add in the ability to adjust the enemy from some generic set of test stats to a specific champion at a specific level with specific items. Phase three, we begin adding more and more champs to the calculator. Ideally, we would like the finished product to be something where you put in how much gold/items yout have, what level you and the enemy is, and what items they have and figure out how much damage you could do with your combo to them. So welcome one and all, and let the 100-0's begin!!
